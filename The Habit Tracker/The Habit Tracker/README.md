The habit tracker is an app that is focused on the monitoring of the habits and goals, this app implements two big functionalities, IA and Coaching these parts will help you to improve your goals an habits. The AI will check your current habits and it will make an improvement and the coaching was created for looking for tutors and coaches that can help you with your goals.


Components: 

In this app i used MVVM architecture to the separation of concerns and responsibilities i also use some Solid principles, i did not use any design pattern. The frameworks i used were SwiftUi and i want to implement SwiftData and Concurrency.
